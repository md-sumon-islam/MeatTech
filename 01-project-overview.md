# Project Overview: MeatTech

MeatTech is an innovative e-commerce and supply chain management platform designed specifically for the meat industry. The platform aims to bridge the gap between livestock farmers, quality meat processors, and end consumers. By leveraging technology, MeatTech ensures that consumers receive fresh, hygienic, and ethically sourced meat right at their doorstep while maintaining absolute transparency in the supply chain.

### Key Objectives
* Provide a seamless online platform for purchasing premium quality meat products.
* Implement strict quality control measures and real-time tracking from farm to fork.
* Empower local farmers by connecting them directly with a broader market.
* Guarantee high standards of food safety and hygiene in processing and logistics..
