# Stakeholder Analysis

| Stakeholder Role | Interests / Needs | Impact on Project |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **End Consumers** | High-quality, fresh, and hygienic meat; timely delivery; transparent sourcing. | High (Primary Users) |
| **Livestock Farmers** | Fair pricing for cattle/poultry; consistent demand; direct market access. | High (Suppliers) |
| **Meat Processors/Butchers** | Standardized working environments; steady supply of animals; strict hygiene protocols. | Medium (Operations) |
| **Delivery Partners** | Optimized routes; fair commission rates; reliable cold-chain logistics equipment. | Medium (Logistics) |
| **Admin / Platform Owner** | Revenue growth; brand reputation; efficient vendor and quality management. | High (Management) |
