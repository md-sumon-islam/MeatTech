# Problem Statement

The traditional meat market suffers from several critical inefficiencies that negatively impact both consumers and producers:

1. **Lack of Freshness and Hygiene:** Most traditional wet markets operate in unhygienic environments, leading to higher risks of bacterial contamination.
2. **No Quality Traceability:** Consumers have no reliable way to verify the origin of the meat, how the livestock was raised, or whether it complies with food safety standards.
3. **Price Manipulation:** Middlemen exploit local livestock farmers by buying at low prices and selling to consumers at inflated rates.
4. **Inconvenience:** Purchasing fresh meat often requires physical visits to markets, which is time-consuming for busy urban populations.

MeatTech solves these problems by establishing a temperature-controlled, direct-to-consumer supply chain that guarantees hygiene, fair pricing, and absolute traceability.
