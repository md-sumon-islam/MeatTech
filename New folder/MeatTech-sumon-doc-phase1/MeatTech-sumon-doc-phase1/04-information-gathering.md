# Information Gathering

To understand the core requirements of the MeatTech platform, the team conducted comprehensive market research using primary and secondary data collection methods:

* **Competitor Analysis:** Evaluated existing meat delivery apps to identify gaps in temperature control tracking and cut customization.
* **Cold-Chain Logistics Research:** Studied optimal temperature requirements (0°C to 4°C for chilled meat) to ensure the technical design supports appropriate logistics tracking.
* **Supply Chain Mapping:** Interviewed local farm managers to map out how livestock transfers from farms to processing centers efficiently.
