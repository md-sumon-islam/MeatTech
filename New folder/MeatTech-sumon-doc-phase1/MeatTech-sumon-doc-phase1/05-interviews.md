# Interview Summary

### Interview 1: Urban Consumer (Target Audience)
* **Participant:** Rahat Karim, 34, Software Engineer.
* **Key Pain Point:** "I want to buy organic and grass-fed beef, but local shops sell mixed meat with no guarantee of origin or age of the meat."
* **Feature Suggestion:** Request for a "Traceability QR Code" on the packaging to scan and view farm details.

### Interview 2: Livestock Farmer
* **Participant:** Abdul Halim, Owner of Halim Cattle Farm.
* **Key Pain Point:** "Middlemen take the massive chunk of profit. During Eid or heavy seasons, market prices fluctuate unfairly."
* **Feature Suggestion:** A transparent dashboard where farmers can see fixed corporate buying rates based on weight and health grade.
