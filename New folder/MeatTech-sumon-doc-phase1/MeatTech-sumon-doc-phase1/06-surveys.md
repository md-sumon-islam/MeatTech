# Survey Results Analysis

A digital survey was conducted with 150 participants from urban areas.

### Key Insights:
* **82%** of respondents expressed concern about the hygiene and safety of meat bought from traditional open markets.
* **65%** are willing to pay a premium price (10-15% higher) if the meat is guaranteed fresh, vacuum-packed, and delivered cold.
* **74%** preferred customized meat cutting options (e.g., curry cut, BBQ cut, mince) over standard bulk blocks.
